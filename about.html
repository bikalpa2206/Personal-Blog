Hello, I'm Bikalpa

Welcome to my little corner of the internet, where travel tales, daily life shenanigans, and the occasional existential crisis collide! Picture me as a blend of Michael Scott and Chandler Bing, navigating the world with a mix of awkward charm and sarcastic wit. Whether I’m getting hilariously lost in a new city or debating if I’m more of a Ross or a Joey in my daily escapades, I promise to keep it real and relatable.

Join me as I share my adventures, from the breathtaking to the downright bizarre, all while trying to figure out what it means to be an adult—spoiler alert: I still don’t have it all figured out! So grab your coffee, kick back, and let’s dive into this chaotic journey together. Just remember, if you’re looking for a perfectly curated travel blog, you might want to check out someone else—I'm here for the laughs and the realness!
